# Outlook

## Automatic Replies

### Vacation

```
Dear Sender,

Thank you for your message. I am out of the office on vacation and will be returning on <DATE>. Please reach out to <BACKUP> if you need urgent assistance. If your message can wait, I will respond to your message in the order in which it has been received upon my return.

Thank you

```

### Unexpected

```
Dear Sender,

Thank you for your message. I am unexpectedly out of the office today. Please reach out to <BACKUP> if you need urgent assistance. If your message can wait, I will respond to your message in the order in which it has been received upon my return.

Thank you

```
